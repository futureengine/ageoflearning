Curriculum & Standards

Age of Learning’s award-winning curriculum is guided and designed by a highly experienced team of educators and other learning experts who have comprehensive knowledge of research-based principles of effective education for children. Our goal: educational excellence for every child. The ABCmouse.com curriculum is also designed to assist teachers throughout the U.S. by aligning with Common Core, individual state standards, and other early learning standards. To review in more detail how ABCmouse.com supports research-based best practices and standards, click to download or view the PDFs below.
