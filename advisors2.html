Business/Strategy & Technology Advisors

Sky Dayton

Mr. Dayton has a long history as a successful entrepreneur in the Internet industry, having founded, co-founded and helped build companies with a combined market value of almost $3 billion.  Best known for founding the Internet Service Provider EarthLink in 1994, Mr. Dayton was also co-founder of Business.com and eCompanies and founder of Boingo Wireless, where he is currently chairman. He also currently serves on several company boards, including Age of Learning, and is a member of the advisory board of the Center for Public Leadership at the John F. Kennedy School of Government at Harvard University.

Dan Rose

Mr. Rose is currently VP of Partnerships at Facebook. As head of global business development and corporate development, he is responsible for strategic partnerships and M&A. He also leads product marketing for Facebook’s monetization efforts, including their innovative advertising products. Mr. Rose joined Facebook in 2006 after seven years at Amazon.com in business development, product management, and digital media development, including the development of the Amazon Kindle. He has a bachelor’s degree from Harvard University.

Dave Goldberg

Mr. Goldberg has been CEO of SurveyMonkey.com since April 2009. His long track record as a successful entrepreneur, venture capitalist, and technology and music industry executive dates back his founding of LAUNCH Media Inc. in 1994. After selling the business to Yahoo in 2001, he served as VP and General Manager of Yahoo! Music. In 2006, Mr. Goldberg was named one of Billboard Magazine’s top power players in digital music. Recently he served as an Entrepreneur in Residence with Benchmark Capital, and currently serves on the Board of Directors at Ancestry.com and Dashbox.



Creative Advisors

Ashley Postlewaite

Ms. Postlewaite is a graduate of Stanford University. Her producing credits include Bugs Bunny in Concert, which enjoyed a highly successful run on Broadway and toured internationally. In 1992, she co-founded Renegade Animation. She has produced feature film animation, episodic television, video games, websites, and a variety of new media applications. She is currently at work on a stage musical adaptation of Christmas Is Here Again, based on the Renegade animated movie. Ms. Postlewaite has served as an international board member and president of the Entrepreneur’s Organization, a global association of business founders.

Darrell Van Citters

Mr. Van Citters is a nationally renowned animation director and the leader of the creative team at Renegade Animation. His directing credits include the television series Captain Sturdy, the Internet series Elmo Aardvark: Outer Space Detective, Hearst’s Little Angels movies, and the current production of The Tom & Jerry Show. Darrell also recently wrote the acclaimed Mr. Magoo’s Christmas Carol, The Making of the First Animated Christmas Special, now in its second printing. He is currently at work on his second book, The Art of Jay Ward.
