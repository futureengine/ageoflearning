Age of Learning makes available the award-winning ABCmouse.com early learning curriculum to as many children, in as many ways, and in as many places as possible. Available for home, school, and library use, on computers, tablets, and smartphones, ABCmouse.com is helping children everywhere build a strong foundation for academic success.

ABCmouse.com Early Learning Academy

Our flagship product is the leading and most comprehensive online early learning resource for children ages 2-6. Children have completed more than 600 million learning activities since the beta launch of ABCmouse.com in 2010. Winner of numerous awards from national education, parent, and media organizations, ABCmouse.com Early Learning Academy offers an expert-designed curriculum for preschool, pre-k, and kindergarten. With more than 5,000 individual learning activities and 450+ complete lessons along our Step-by-Step Learning Path™, ABCmouse.com encompasses reading and language arts, math, science, social studies, art and music.
