Careers

ABCmouse.com is the global education initiative of Age of Learning, Inc. Our goal is to help build a strong foundation for future academic success by providing a comprehensive and engaging online curriculum that greatly assists young children to succeed in pre–k, kindergarten, early elementary school, and beyond.

Age of Learning, Inc. is looking for passionate individuals who can bring their talents to the world of early childhood education. We strive to bring meaningful education into children’s lives every day and are looking for the best and the brightest to help us accomplish this goal.

Here at ABCmouse.com, we encourage an open and lively work environment: from our monthly, company-wide birthday celebrations; all-staff field trips to screen the latest animated feature films; to the occasional playful NERF® wars in the studio, team members come to work knowing they can have fun in a highly creative yet productive atmosphere.

If you would like to find out more about how you might play a role on our highly productive, dedicated team—all while making a long-term difference in the lives of young children throughout the world—we encourage you to tell us about yourself and how you would like to contribute!

    We are looking for an artist who will be responsible for the conceptualization and execution of illustrated and/or animated props, animals, and characters for online use.

    Qualifications:

    Must be highly skilled in Flash animation, with a strong sense of timing and appeal while keeping animations clear and on model

    Ability to utilize a variety of Flash techniques as pertaining to animation, including tweening, nesting, the use of symbols, etc.

    Must be skilled in Flash illustration, including quick and efficient sketches, clear composition, strong inking ability and a strong sense of color

    Ability to maintain brand identity throughout work, following established guidelines and drawing on model as required

    Ability to work collaboratively within a production pipeline

    Must have a willingness to learn and the ability to take direction and make quick adjustments to artwork as needed

    Ability to adapt to a range of animation and illustration styles, including both cartoon and realistic, for a variety of subjects including the human figure, animals, props, backgrounds, etc.

    Must demonstrate an advanced level of proficiency in Flash, as well as proficiency in Photoshop and Illustrator

    Strong communication skills

    Ability to work within deadlines and switch between priorities as needed

    Only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted. Eligible employees must be able to take a full-time, in-house position. No telecommuting.

Candidates must include a link to their portfolio or provide work samples as part of the application process. Only complete submissions will be considered.
