Careers


What could be better?

Join a team that led the creation of the most engaging youth entertainment website of its time (Neopets.com) and is now developing and expanding a range of ground-breaking educational resources for young children: ABCmouse.com, education and language-learning apps, and other projects in development. Per independent research studies, our curriculum is greatly impacting learning outcomes with young learners in homes, schools and other learning centers across the U.S. and around the world, with more than 600 million learning activities completed to date.

Come collaborate with a passionate and talented group of dedicated, creative professionals—from education experts, developers, artists, writers, and designers to producers, product managers, and marketing experts—at our rapidly growing company based in the Los Angeles area.

If you think you can help us make a difference in the lives of young children throughout the world, we encourage you to tell us about yourself and how you can contribute. Together, let’s bring learning to life!
